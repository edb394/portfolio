Space Wars

Mission objective

Primarily, “Space Wars” is an interactive comic. It aims to entertain the reader and offer compelling visuals to guide them along the story. Here, an emphasis is placed on the visual and interactive elements so there is little to no friction when the story is being read.


At a glance

Front-end Web Dev, Graphic Design

Tools

Illustrator CC, Photoshop CC, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Development

After conceiving the idea as a group and sketching out a draft storyboard, I started developing the high-visuals immediately. Adding interactive elements to a story was tricky, but it keeps the reader engaged and satisfied.
